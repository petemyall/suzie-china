Day 1: Arrival in Shanghai

We’ve arrived in Shanghai to a warm, muggy day — and what a welcome! The city is alive with energy, its skyline glowing into the night with soaring high-rise towers and dazzling lights.

From our hotel room we’re treated to a breathtaking view of the Oriental Pearl Tower, standing at an impressive 1,500 feet — the 5th tallest in the world. Watching it sparkle against the night sky feels like stepping right into the heart of modern China.

Tomorrow promises plenty of adventure, but for now we’re a little jet-lagged and soaking up the excitement (and maybe a power nap or two 😆).
