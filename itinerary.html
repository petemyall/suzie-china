Grand Tour of China

苏茜在中国的奇遇记

Tour Highlights

Explore the majestic Great Wall and the imperial splendour of the Forbidden City in Beijing, then journey to Xi’an to witness the awe-inspiring Terracotta Warriors. Drift through the dreamlike karst peaks along the Li River in Guilin, and visit the lovable Giant Pandas in Chengdu—or trek the misty cliffs and glass walkways of Zhangjiajie. Enjoy a tranquil boat ride on Hangzhou’s West Lake, sip tea in a traditional Suzhou garden, and take a high-speed train ride across the countryside. Discover the colourful markets of Lijiang, walk the ancient city walls of Pingyao, and watch the spectacular Impression Liu Sanjie light show. Finally, soak up the vibrant contrast of modern skylines and timeless temples in dazzling Shanghai.

Day-by-Day Itinerary

  • Day 1: Arrive in Beijing.
  • Day 2: Tiananmen Square, Forbidden City, Peking Duck dinner.
  • Day 3: Great Wall at Mutianyu, Summer Palace.
  • Day 4: High-speed train to Xi’an.
  • Day 5: Terracotta Army, Muslim Quarter, Xi’an Wall.
  • Day 6: Flight/train to Zhangjiajie or Chengdu.
  • Day 7: Explore Zhangjiajie Forest Park or Panda Research Base.
  • Day 8: Travel to Guilin, relax by the river.
  • Day 9: Li River cruise to Yangshuo, overnight stay.
  • Day 10: Cycle around Yangshuo, see countryside villages.
  • Day 11: Train or flight to Shanghai.
  • Day 12: Visit The Bund, Yu Garden, Nanjing Road.
  • Day 13: Day trip to Zhujiajiao water town or free time.
  • Day 14: Final shopping, flight home.
