Tour Highlights

Explore the majestic Great Wall and the imperial splendour of the Forbidden City in Beijing, then journey to Xi’an to witness the awe-inspiring Terracotta Warriors. Drift through the dreamlike karst peaks along the Li River in Guilin, and visit the lovable Giant Pandas in Chengdu—or trek the misty cliffs and glass walkways of Zhangjiajie. Enjoy a tranquil boat ride on Hangzhou’s West Lake, sip tea in a traditional Suzhou garden, and take a high-speed train ride across the countryside. Discover the colourful markets of Lijiang, walk the ancient city walls of Pingyao, and watch the spectacular Impression Liu Sanjie light show. Finally, soak up the vibrant contrast of modern skylines and timeless temples in dazzling Shanghai.

Join Suzie and Jazz on Their China Adventure

Mother and son team, Suzie and Jazz, are about to set off on the journey of a lifetime — a whirlwind adventure through some of China’s most iconic cities and landscapes. Their travels begin in the dazzling modern skyline of Shanghai, before heading inland to the dramatic sandstone pillars of Zhangjiajie, a natural wonder that looks like it was plucked straight from a fantasy film set.

From there, they continue west to Chengdu, where China’s most charming residents — the giant pandas — are waiting to be admired in their natural habitat. The journey then moves north to Xi’an, home to the awe-inspiring Terracotta Warriors, standing in silent formation as they have for over 2,000 years.

Finally, Suzie and Jazz will conclude their clockwise loop in the capital, Beijing, exploring imperial palaces, ancient temples, and the breathtaking Great Wall of China. Together, this itinerary blends old and new, nature and culture, creating memories that will last a lifetime.

Stay tuned as Suzie and Jazz share their stories, snapshots, and surprises from this unforgettable adventure.
