Learn Mandarin Basics

Heading to China? A few key Mandarin phrases can go a long way — and Suzie’s been practicing! Here’s a handy cheat sheet to get you through markets, temples, taxis, and noodle stalls.

Essential Phrases

  • 你好 (Nǐ hǎo) – Hello
  • 谢谢 (Xièxiè) – Thank you
  • 请问 (Qǐngwèn) – Excuse me / May I ask
  • 对不起 (Duìbuqǐ) – Sorry
  • 多少钱? (Duōshǎo qián?) – How much is it?
  • 厕所在哪里? (Cèsuǒ zài nǎlǐ?) – Where is the toilet?
  • 好吃! (Hǎochī!) – Delicious!
  • 再见 (Zàijiàn) – Goodbye
Practice with a smile — even a little Mandarin brings big smiles in return 🇨🇳😊
